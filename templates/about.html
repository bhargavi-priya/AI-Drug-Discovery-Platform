About This Project


The AI Drug Discovery Platform is a bioinformatics web application developed using Flask, BioPython and RDKit.


It enables researchers and students to upload protein and drug files, analyze protein sequences, calculate molecular descriptors, evaluate drug-likeness using Lipinski's Rule of Five, visualize molecular structures, and generate downloadable PDF reports.
